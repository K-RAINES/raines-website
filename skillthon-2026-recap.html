Event Recap

K-RAINES Skillthon 2026

From ideas to working demos with agentic AI. A hands-on community event where participants used AI agents to turn domain knowledge, rough concepts, and unfinished ideas into live presentations.

Date
May 21, 2026
Location
CIC Kendall Square, Cambridge, MA
Format
Quick-start, build session, demos, judging, awards, networking

K-RAINES Skillthon 2026 brought together builders, professionals, students, and community members to test how agentic AI tools can help move an idea into something concrete.

14 advance registrations
7 final presentations
3 award placements
1 afternoon of building

A Different Kind of Hackathon

The event was designed not simply as a technical competition, but as a hands-on space where participants could explore whether Codex-style workflows could turn their own problems and ideas into working prototypes.

The final group was smaller and more conversational than a crowded demo day. Presenters had time to explain what they built, answer questions, and share the thinking behind each project.

What stood out most was how participants used AI as a collaborator while still talking with others, asking questions, refining ideas, and staying present in the room.

이번 행사는 단순한 코딩 속도 경쟁이 아니라, 각자의 문제의식과 도메인 경험을 AI 에이전트와 함께 실제 데모로 바꿔보는 실전형 커뮤니티 행사였습니다.

The strongest theme was translation: turning experience, expertise, and curiosity into something others could see, react to, and discuss.
